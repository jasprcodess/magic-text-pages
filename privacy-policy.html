Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Magic Text ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data remains on your device:

  • Videos you import are stored locally on your device only
  • Text overlays you create are stored locally on your device
  • In-app purchase information is handled by Apple - we do not have access to your payment details

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, share, or process any personal data. All video editing and text creation happens entirely on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.
  • Analytics: We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage and Security

All content you create (videos, text overlays, exported videos) is stored only on your device. We do not have access to your content. Your data is as secure as your device's security settings.

5. User Content and Copyright

You retain all rights to content you create using Magic Text. You are responsible for ensuring you have the rights to any videos you import into the app. If you believe content created with our app infringes your copyright, please contact our DMCA agent at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Magic Text does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since all data remains on the device and we do not collect any information, the app is safe for use by children under parental supervision.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All content is stored on your device
  • You can delete individual videos within the app
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device
