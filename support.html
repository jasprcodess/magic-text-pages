Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock premium effects?
Go to Settings → Unlock Premium Effects. You can purchase all premium effects for a one-time payment of $2.99. No subscription required!
Where are my exported videos saved?
All exported videos are saved to your Photos app automatically. You can find them in your Camera Roll or Recents album.
Can I use multiple text layers?
Yes! Tap the "+ Text" button to add as many text layers as you want to your video.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your premium unlock is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.
Why isn't my text showing on the exported video?
Make sure your text is visible on the video preview before exporting. If the issue persists, please contact support with details about your device and iOS version.
What video formats are supported?
Magic Text supports all standard video formats including MP4, MOV, and M4V. Videos are exported in high-quality MP4 format.
Is my data private?
Yes! All videos are processed locally on your device. We never upload, access, or store your content. Your privacy is our priority.
Can I request a refund?
All purchases are handled through Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Refunds are subject to Apple's refund policy.
